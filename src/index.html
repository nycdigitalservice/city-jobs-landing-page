Recruitment events

Discover opportunities and learn about civil service. At some events, you can come with a resume and leave with a job.

    Civil service exams

    Some City jobs may require you to take an exam, either before or after getting hired. These jobs are classified as competitive on the job board. Signing up for an upcoming exam is a great way to land a new job in your field.
